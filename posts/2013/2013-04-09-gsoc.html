Google Summer of Code¶

SunPy is participating in the Google Summer of Code 2013 under the umbrella of the Python Software Foundation .

We have participated succefully in the ESA summer of code in space project which is similar to GSoC for the last two years. Both our students and our project have gained a lot from the experience. A list of project ideas and application info is on our GitHub Wiki .

If you are interested in talking to us about projects, applications or if you are just bored pop into our IRC channel #SunPy on irc.freenode.net or drop us a line on our mailing list .

Enjoy your summer of code!
